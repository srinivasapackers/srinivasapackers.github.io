Services

We provide Packing, Loading, Moving, Transportation, Unloading, Unpacking, Rearrangement and Insurance coverage for your goods. We provide House shifting, Office relocation, Domestic shifting and Local shifting. We also offer car carrier services, four wheeler transport and two wheeler transport.

Packing

We collect all your possessions from your house or office and pack them within few hours.

  • All glass items are packed using grass and thermocol sheets
  • Iron safe and other large items are packed using corrugated sheet
  • Medium size items such as TV, fridge, washing machine etc, are packed in their original boxes if available, else they will be packed in our own boxes or using corrugated sheets
  • Sofa set etc., are packed using polythene sheets
  • Miscellaneous items which include kitchen items, crockery, clothes are packed in carton boxes
  • Crockery is packed using cutting waste paper

Loading

Our expert team of workers load your belongings for transport with atmost care. We take all measures to ensure protection of your belongings from water, rain, heat etc.

Transportation (Moving)

We have a team of skilled drivers who reach your belongings safely to your destination. All local shiftings are done in a lorry, TATA 407. And shiftings to outside city are done in a truck, Eicher.

Unloading

We unload all the goods after transporting them to your destination. On reaching the destination, our expert workers unload and unpack all the possessions without wasting a point of time. You can keep your hands free.

Unpacking and Rearranging

After unloading the goods we remove all the packing material and other stuff used for packing. Also we rearrange all the major items at your desired location. Thus we save a lot of your valuable time and energy.

Relocation Services

We offer House shifting, Office relocation, Domestic shifting and Local shifting. We are the largest movers and packers in Vijayawada.

  • House shifting: We shift your household items
  • Office relocation: We also offer corporate shifting
  • Domestic shifting: We offer our relocation services all over India
  • Local shifting: We offer local shifting in Vijayawada
